About Me

"Everything is designed. Few things are designed well."

In today’s world, everything around us is designed — from the phones we use to the chairs we sit on, even the apps we scroll through daily. Design is not limited to visual beauty; it's about how something works, how it feels, and how it solves a problem.

However, not all design is thoughtful or effective. Many products may look appealing but fail in functionality, usability, or purpose. That’s why well-designed things stand out — they are intuitive, user-friendly, and serve their purpose beautifully.
